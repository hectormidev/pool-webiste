Maintenance and Cleaning

Welcome to H&R POOL SERVICES , your trusted partner in pool care and maintenance. With a passion for pristine waters and a commitment to exceptional service, we take pride in transforming pools into sparkling havens of relaxation and enjoyment.

services

Here's what we offer:

Water Testing

Our precise seven-point water test provides accurate readings of your pool's chemical levels. This ensures your water stays clear, healthy, and safe for both swimmers and your pool's equipment and finish.

Pool Shocking

Weekly shocking is essential in eliminating organic contaminants. We use top-quality products like Suncoast Gold Liquid Chlorine or Salinity Surge shock to keep your pool water pristine.

Water Balance

We maintain your water's clarity and prevent algae growth with our exclusive line of Suncoast Chemicals. Your pool will remain inviting and algae-free. Sanitization: We use Suncoast Complete chlorine tablets or Salinity Pool Salt to keep your pool sanitized and safe for swimming all year round.

Basket Emptying

Prevent clogging and ensure smooth equipment operation by removing debris from pump and skimmer baskets regularly. Filter Cleaning: No matter the type of filter, we've got it covered. We offer backwashing, thorough cleaning, and even complete media replacement for sand, cartridge, and DE filters.

Pool Brushing:

Our experts meticulously brush pool walls, tile, waterline, and steps. This proactive step helps prevent the buildup of grime, algae, and calcium deposits.

Pool Equipment Installation & Repair

  • Pool Equipment Installation, Service, and Repair:

    Our licensed experts are equipped to handle all your pool and spa equipment needs. Whether it's installation, routine maintenance, or urgent repairs, we've got you covered.

Pool Leak Detection & Repair

Is your swimming pool experiencing unexplained water loss? Leaks can occur for various reasons and in different areas of your pool. A leak not only disrupts chemical balance but also results in unnecessary water and financial loss.

Why Trust Us for Pool Leak Detection and Repair:

Precise Leak Detection: Our highly-trained technicians specialize in pinpointing the exact location of leaks, ensuring a swift resolution. Customized Solutions: Once the leak is identified, our experts will present you with tailored repair options based on factors like the size, type, and location of the leak. Timely Repairs: We understand the urgency of fixing a leak. Our team is committed to resolving the issue as quickly as possible to minimize any further inconvenience. Cost-Efficient Solutions: We provide transparent details regarding repair costs and procedures, so you can make informed decisions without any hidden surprises. Protect Your Investment: A leak-free pool not only saves you money on water but also ensures proper chemical balance, preserving the longevity of your pool.
